# Hero Slideshow Images

Add your 5 dental clinic images here with these names:

- `hero1.jpg`
- `hero2.jpg`
- `hero3.jpg`
- `hero4.jpg`
- `hero5.jpg`

## Image Requirements

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Size:** 1920x1080px (Full HD) recommended
- **File size:** Keep under 500KB for fast loading
- **Content ideas:**
  - Dental clinic exterior
  - Modern waiting room
  - Dental chair/treatment room
  - Happy patients with dentists
  - Team photo

## How to Optimize Images

Before adding images, compress them:
- Use [TinyPNG.com](https://tinypng.com) or [Squoosh.app](https://squoosh.app)
- This keeps your site fast!

## Current Status

The hero section will show a gradient background until you add images here.
The slideshow will automatically start once images are added.
